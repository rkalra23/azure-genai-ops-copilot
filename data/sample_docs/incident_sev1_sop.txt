Title: Severity 1 Incident Handling SOP
Version: v2
Effective Date: 2025-11-10
Department: SRE
Doc Type: sop

Overview:
This SOP defines the process for handling Severity 1 incidents.

Procedure:
1. Declare Severity 1 incident.
2. Create incident bridge within 5 minutes.
3. Notify stakeholders and leadership.
4. Assign incident commander.
5. Begin mitigation and status updates every 15 minutes.
6. Document actions and timeline.
7. Start post-incident review within 2 business days.

Escalation:
Escalate to senior leadership if customer impact exceeds 30 minutes.
